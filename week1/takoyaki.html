Daiki's Recipe #1

Takoyaki (Japanese Octopus Balls)

2 servings

Ingredients

  • 500g of Takoyaki Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 500ml of Water
  • 100g of Green Onions
  • 50g of Pickled Ginger
  • 75g of Octupus
  • 20ml of Olive Oil
  • Mayonnaise
  • Japanese Takoyaki Sauce

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix Takoyaki flour, two eggs, and water.
  2. Chop green oninons, pickled ginger, and octopus in small pieces.
  3. Turn on Takoyaki maker, put olive oil in each ball, and wait until it's hot.
  4. Put the flour mixture into the balls.
  5. Add chopped ingredients into the batter that is being cooked in the balls.
  6. When the batter gets brown, use a toothpick to flip it over.
  7. When fully cooked, put Takoyaki onto a plate and serve with mayonnaise and Japanese Takoyaki sause.
